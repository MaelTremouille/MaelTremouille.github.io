Profile

Final-year engineering student at École polytechnique and ENSAE Paris, specializing in data science and statistics. My current research asks how factual updates are represented and accessed across languages in large language models. More broadly, I am drawn to causal accounts of model behavior and to interventions that remain reliable across tasks, languages, and contexts.
