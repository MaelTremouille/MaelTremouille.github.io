About

I am drawn to problems where mathematical clarity meets systems that matter in practice.

I began in statistics and computer science, then moved toward deep learning and language models. I enjoy the full research loop: reading papers, formulating experiments, building reliable code, and making sense of results.

What keeps me interested is the gap between a model working and understanding why it works. I care about explanations that survive careful testing, and software that makes those tests reproducible.
